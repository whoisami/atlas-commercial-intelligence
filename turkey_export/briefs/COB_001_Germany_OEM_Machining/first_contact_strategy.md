## Gating sequence

2. **Manufacturer before buyer.** If a target reaches `PROCEED`, the first contact is with the Turkish manufacturer (Pro-Mak or Kurutlu), not the buyer platform. Atlas needs the manufacturer's explicit authorization and mandate before referencing the manufacturer in any buyer-facing context.
3. **Manufacturer authorization gate.** Contact with Pro-Mak/Kurutlu is limited to: confirming interest in export/BD support, testing appetite for a paid advisory engagement, and obtaining permission to prepare (not submit) a capability pack. No submission, application or platform registration is authorized at this stage.
4. **Platform engagement only after manufacturer mandate.** Only after the manufacturer authorizes it would Atlas assist with a platform application (e.g., Xometry Türkiye's partner registration) — and even then, the manufacturer, not Atlas, is the registering legal entity, per each platform's own terms.
