# Commercial Opportunity Brief #001 — Demand-First Implementation of SPEC_002

- **Specification implemented:** [`specs/02_demand_signal_engine.md`](../../specs/02_demand_signal_engine.md)
- **Method:** Started from commercial demand (Turkish defense-industrial export growth, Gulf and NATO-ally supply-chain diversification), not from a manufacturer list, per the Discovery Rules in this brief's instructions and `ATLAS_RULES.md`'s demand-first doctrine.
- **Region:** Europe and Middle East
- **Evidence cutoff:** 2026-07-06
- **External contact made:** 0

## What this brief does

1. Establishes a real, dated, multi-source demand narrative: Gulf-state ammunition/component shortages and NATO-ally supply-chain diversification toward Turkish defense-industrial capacity.
2. Works backward from that demand to identify the one Atlas-known Turkish manufacturer with the strongest, most independently verifiable fit — **ERBAB Otomat Yedek Parça Sanayi Ticaret A.Ş.** (Bursa).
3. Independently verifies ERBAB across three source classes beyond its own website: HOSAB (Hasanağa Organized Industrial Zone registry), SAHA İstanbul (Turkey's largest defense/aerospace industry cluster), and third-party trade/industry directories.
4. Analyzes ERBAB's export growth gaps, realistic buyer/sourcing/distributor/representation opportunities, Atlas service opportunities, revenue model options, risks and next commercial action.
5. Presents a CEO Review answering the required questions directly, in [`executive_summary.md`](executive_summary.md).

## Why ERBAB and not a new/unverified manufacturer

ERBAB already carries defense-relevant certifications (National-NATO Facility Security Certificate, AS9100, IATF 16949) and cluster membership (SAHA İstanbul) — independently corroborated, not company-only claims — that directly match the demand narrative in [`target_company.md`](target_company.md). No newly discovered manufacturer in this session matched the demand narrative with comparable independent verification within this brief's research bound.
