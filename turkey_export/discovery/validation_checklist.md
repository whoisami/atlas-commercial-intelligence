# Manufacturer Validation Checklist

Complete this checklist before a record enters any ranked output.

## Identity and manufacturing

- [ ] Company name and Turkish operating location are verified.
- [ ] Official website is active and belongs to the same entity.
- [ ] Direct evidence supports real manufacturing activity.
- [ ] The company fits at least one allowed OEM Metal Components subsector.
- [ ] Distributor, trader and marketplace status has been excluded.

## Capability evidence

- [ ] Capabilities are supported by process, product or technical pages.
- [ ] Machinery evidence is recorded or explicitly `UNKNOWN`.
- [ ] Industries served are supported or explicitly `UNKNOWN`.
- [ ] Product-complexity and repeat-order claims are explained.

## Export and quality

- [ ] Export evidence or a specific export-readiness signal is recorded.
- [ ] Foreign-language readiness is assessed without treating translation alone as export proof.
- [ ] Certifications are verified where possible; unverified claims are labeled.
- [ ] Public contact channel is usable or marked `UNKNOWN`.

## Evidence integrity

- [ ] Every company has direct source URLs.
- [ ] Source URLs support the fields they are cited for.
- [ ] Conflicting evidence is resolved or described.
- [ ] Unknown data is written as `UNKNOWN`.
- [ ] No personal, private or invented information is present.

## Scoring and status

- [ ] All ten scoring criteria have an evidence-based score.
- [ ] Discovery Confidence is calculated independently.
- [ ] Export Potential Score and Atlas Score recalculate correctly.
- [ ] Score rationale explains positive evidence, deductions and unknowns.
- [ ] `PROCEED` passes every hard gate.
- [ ] `WAIT` names the next verification action.
- [ ] `DROP` names the disqualifier.

## Portfolio output

- [ ] Duplicate companies and group entities are reconciled.
- [ ] Top 100 contains only qualified discovered manufacturers.
- [ ] Top 20 contains only export-ready `PROCEED` manufacturers.
- [ ] Top 5 contains only immediate candidates with sufficient public data.
- [ ] Every `PROCEED` candidate has a specific reason.
- [ ] No company was contacted and no outreach email was created.
