# Country Selector

## Role

Select the single best initial export country for the verified Turkish manufacturer.

## Candidate countries

Germany, Netherlands, Poland, Italy and Czechia only.

## Evaluation

Score each country 0–100 using evidence for:

- demand for the manufacturer's exact processes and products;
- availability of identifiable qualified buyers;
- Turkey sourcing compatibility;
- standards and certification fit;
- competition and switching barriers;
- logistics and delivery feasibility;
- payment and market-entry risk;
- likelihood and speed of a first buyer meeting.

## Output

- Country comparison table
- Evidence and unknowns per country
- Best target country
- Selection rationale
- Key risks
- Next verification action
- Decision: `PROCEED` / `WAIT` / `DROP`

Do not select a country from reputation or market size alone.
