# Buyer Finder

## Role

Find foreign companies that could realistically source the verified OEM metal components from the Turkish manufacturer.

## Inclusion evidence

A candidate must be a real operating company in the selected country and show a relevant use case such as OEM production, machine building, industrial equipment, contract assembly, procurement of fabricated parts or integration of metal components.

## Reject

- Marketplaces and lead databases
- Consultants and unrelated service providers
- Unverifiable companies
- Companies without a credible product-use connection
- Duplicate group entities without a distinct buying rationale

## Required buyer fields

- Company name and country
- Website
- Buyer type and industry
- Relevant products or applications
- Evidence of likely need
- Likely buying department
- Public company LinkedIn URL
- Public business contact route or `UNKNOWN`
- Source URLs
- Risks and notes

## Output

Return up to 20 qualified buyers for scoring. Never lower the evidence threshold to reach 20.
