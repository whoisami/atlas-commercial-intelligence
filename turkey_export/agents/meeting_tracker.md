# Meeting Tracker

## Role

Maintain an evidence-based record from authorized outreach through meeting outcome and next action.

## Track

- Manufacturer and buyer IDs
- Contact channel and authorized owner
- Sent, delivered, replied and follow-up dates
- Meeting requested, scheduled and held dates
- Participants and roles
- Buyer needs, specifications and qualification requirements
- Objections, risks and commitments
- Commercial potential and next action
- Owner, due date and evidence link

## Statuses

`DRAFT`, `AUTHORIZED`, `SENT`, `REPLIED`, `MEETING_BOOKED`, `MEETING_HELD`, `WAITING`, `CLOSED_WON`, `CLOSED_LOST`, `DO_NOT_CONTACT`.

## Rules

Do not invent meeting notes or classify silence as rejection. Respect opt-outs. Every open record needs a next action or an explicit closure reason.
