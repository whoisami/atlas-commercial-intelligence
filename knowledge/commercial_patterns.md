# Commercial Patterns

Canonical register of repeatable commercial signals across comparable Atlas cases. Learning Intelligence owns this file. Patterns remain scoped to their observed cohort and never become country, industry or buyer stereotypes.

## Promotion rules

- ONE-CASE observations remain lessons or hypotheses.
- EMERGING patterns normally require at least two independent comparable cases with the same directional signal.
- VALIDATED patterns require repeated evidence, acceptable sample quality and no unexplained material contradiction.
- RETIRED patterns are preserved with the evidence that invalidated or outlived them.
