# SOP — Buyer Intelligence

## Workflow

1. Define the ideal customer profile and excluded account types.
2. Build a candidate universe from official facility, association and project sources.
3. Verify each company’s Turkish operation and relevant process.
4. Map product application, maintenance cycle and likely buying unit.
5. Find procurement or technical roles from official sources.
6. Score demand, access, timing, trust and commercial fit.
7. Recommend GO/WATCH/NO-GO and next research action.
8. Write buyer report and proposed buyer delta.

## Escalation

Stop and escalate to Atlas CEO when the requested action exceeds this agent's authority, evidence is contradictory on a decisive point, a source suggests sanctions or legal risk, or completion would require contacting an external party without authorization.

## Completion definition

Work is complete only when the report is saved in the correct intelligence domain, every decisive claim has a source, unknowns are explicit, status logic is explained, proposed database/dashboard changes are stated, and the next action has an owner.
