# Checklist — Buyer Intelligence

## Start gate

- [ ] Market Opportunity report is accepted and GO.
- [ ] Product Intelligence report is accepted and GO.
- [ ] Product boundaries, applications and buyer roles are explicit.
- [ ] OEM, project, aftermarket and MRO routes are understood.
- [ ] Buyer Intelligence is not re-running market selection.
- [ ] Buyer Intelligence is not researching manufacturers.

## Ideal customer profiles

- [ ] Applicable buyer types are defined separately.
- [ ] Each profile has a qualifying operation and application.
- [ ] Purchase trigger and repeat mechanism are defined.
- [ ] User, specifier and procurement route are defined.
- [ ] Exclusion rules are explicit.
- [ ] Dealer, EPC, service, OEM and end-user roles are not conflated.

## Entity and operation verification

- [ ] Company identity is verified.
- [ ] Turkish operation is verified.
- [ ] City is verified.
- [ ] Facility or operational unit is identified.
- [ ] Parent, subsidiary, brand and branch relationships are resolved.
- [ ] Website and LinkedIn company page match the entity.
- [ ] Duplicates and closed/stale operations are removed.

## Demand qualification

- [ ] Potential Product Need is specific.
- [ ] Reason They May Buy is tied to verified operations.
- [ ] Demand is classified as Verified, Strong Hypothesis, Weak or No Fit.
- [ ] Purchase trigger is identified or UNKNOWN.
- [ ] Direct demand evidence is separated from inference.
- [ ] Contradictory evidence is recorded.
- [ ] Industry membership alone is not treated as demand.
- [ ] Every included account explains why it may buy.

## Purchase behavior

- [ ] Buyer route is classified.
- [ ] Estimated Purchase Frequency is sourced or UNKNOWN.
- [ ] No order frequency or quantity was invented.
- [ ] Planned, project, shutdown, tender and emergency demand are separated.
- [ ] OEM and aftermarket behavior are distinguished.
- [ ] Qualification or framework constraints are identified.

## Buying center

- [ ] User department is identified or UNKNOWN.
- [ ] Specifier is identified or UNKNOWN.
- [ ] Technical approver is identified or UNKNOWN.
- [ ] Quality/compliance role is identified or UNKNOWN.
- [ ] Budget owner is identified or UNKNOWN.
- [ ] Procurement/purchasing is identified or UNKNOWN.
- [ ] Payer/legal entity is identified or UNKNOWN.
- [ ] Department mapping precedes person-level research.

## Public contact and privacy

- [ ] Purchasing Contact is public or UNKNOWN.
- [ ] Professional role is relevant and sourced.
- [ ] Email is public or UNKNOWN.
- [ ] Phone is public or UNKNOWN.
- [ ] Source URL and verification date are recorded.
- [ ] No email or phone pattern was inferred.
- [ ] No private, gated or sensitive personal data was collected.
- [ ] No access control was bypassed.
- [ ] No contact was made.

## Commercial scoring

- [ ] Need Score is 0–100.
- [ ] Buying Probability is 0–100.
- [ ] Repeat Purchase Potential is 0–100.
- [ ] Strategic Importance is 0–100.
- [ ] Ease of Contact is 0–100.
- [ ] Market Influence is 0–100.
- [ ] Weights total 100%.
- [ ] Priority Score arithmetic is verified.
- [ ] Evidence Quality is scored separately.
- [ ] Every score has evidence, confidence and unknowns.
- [ ] Unsupported criteria are labeled UNKNOWN and scored 0.

## GO gate

- [ ] Priority Score ≥ 75.
- [ ] Need Score ≥ 70.
- [ ] Buying Probability ≥ 60.
- [ ] Evidence Quality ≥ 70.
- [ ] Turkish operation is verified.
- [ ] Buyer type is verified.
- [ ] Product need and reason to buy are specific.
- [ ] Decision-maker department is identified.
- [ ] Public company response channel exists.
- [ ] No disqualifying contradiction remains.

If any box fails, the account cannot be GO.

## Portfolio quality

- [ ] Top 100 contains only qualified accounts.
- [ ] A shortfall below 100 is explained rather than padded.
- [ ] Top 25 prioritizes demand and probability, not company fame.
- [ ] Top 10 contains only GO accounts.
- [ ] Top 10 has a department and public response route.
- [ ] Top 10 has a first-contact hypothesis and risk note.
- [ ] Rankings are consistent across buyer types.
- [ ] Accounts included only for industry adjacency are rejected.

## Outputs

- [ ] Report uses the next `intelligence/buyers/RXXX.md`.
- [ ] Report ends with the six mandatory sections in order.
- [ ] `database/buyers.csv` matches the report.
- [ ] `workspace/active_project.md` matches the pipeline and next gate.
- [ ] Only Buyer Intelligence dashboard values were updated.
- [ ] Stable IDs, unrelated content and source history were preserved.
- [ ] Every UNKNOWN names the next verification.
- [ ] Top 10 handoff is complete.
- [ ] No outreach was authorized or performed.

## Integrity

- [ ] No company, facility, need, project, contact, email, phone, frequency or purchase intent was invented.
- [ ] Public information only was used.
- [ ] Every recommendation contains evidence.
- [ ] Every included buyer has an explicit reason to buy.
