# KPIs — Buyer Intelligence

## North-star outcome

**Share of Top 10 Immediate Targets that validate the product need in a qualified commercial conversation after authorized outreach.**

The agent is measured on demand accuracy and pipeline quality—not the number of companies collected.

## Demand-quality KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target |
|---|---|---:|
| Need validation rate | GO buyers whose need is confirmed in later contact | ≥ 70% |
| False-demand rate | GO buyers rejected because public evidence available at research time disproved need | ≤ 10% |
| Account explanation coverage | Included buyers with specific reason to buy | 100% |
| Turkish-operation verification | GO buyers with verified Turkish operation and city | 100% |
| Buyer-type accuracy | Accounts later confirmed in the assigned buying role | ≥ 90% |
| Evidence Quality at GO | GO accounts meeting evidence threshold | 100% |

## Buying-center KPIs

- GO accounts with decision-maker department identified: **100%**
- Top 10 with public company response route: **100%**
- Named contacts with publicly sourced professional role: **100%**
- Inferred personal emails or phones: **0**
- Buying-center corrections after outreach: track and reduce quarterly.

## Pipeline KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target |
|---|---|---:|
| Top 10 qualification rate | Top 10 remaining valid after CEO/outreach review | ≥ 90% |
| Buyer-to-meeting conversion | Top 10 reaching qualified meetings after authorized outreach | Track by cohort; improve quarterly |
| Repeat-demand validation | GO accounts with confirmed recurring mechanism | ≥ 80% |
| Manufacturer-value usefulness | Manufacturer conversations where buyer evidence improves credibility or response | Track by sprint |
| Pipeline concentration risk | Top 10 overconcentrated in one group, city or buyer type without rationale | 0 unexplained cases |

## Scoring integrity

- GO accounts with Priority Score ≥ 75: **100%**
- GO accounts with Need Score ≥ 70: **100%**
- GO accounts with Buying Probability ≥ 60: **100%**
- GO accounts with Evidence Quality ≥ 70: **100%**
- Priority Score arithmetic errors: **0**
- Unsupported criteria scored above zero without explanation: **0**

## Research-efficiency KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target |
|---|---|---:|
| Time to qualified pipeline | Product Intelligence GO to final buyer report | ≤ 10 business days unless scope requires otherwise |
| Early rejection efficiency | Weak relevance/no-fit accounts removed before deep contact research | ≥ 90% |
| Deep-review yield | Deep-reviewed accounts reaching GO or bounded WATCH | ≥ 60% |
| List-padding rate | Accounts added only to reach 100 | 0 |
| Duplicate account rate | Unresolved duplicates in canonical database | 0 |
| Open-ended WATCH rate | WATCH without verification owner and trigger | 0 |

## Privacy and data-quality guardrails

- Private or gated personal information collected: **0**
- Access controls bypassed: **0**
- Invented companies, needs, projects, contacts or purchase intent: **0**
- Records without source URLs: **0**
- UNKNOWN values without impact and next verification: **0**
- Unauthorized outreach: **0**

## Downstream feedback

Outreach Intelligence and Atlas CEO must record:

- Contact validity.
- Whether the department was correct.
- Whether product need was confirmed.
- Buying timeframe and purchase route.
- Existing supplier or framework constraint.
- Reason for rejection, delay or meeting.
- Whether the account influences manufacturer interest.

Learning Intelligence uses these outcomes to recalibrate scores and buyer archetypes.

## Review cadence

- Validate all GO gates before report closure.
- Review Top 10 after contact enrichment.
- Review demand predictions after first response and meeting.
- Recalibrate buyer-type and scoring assumptions quarterly.
- Never lower evidence standards merely to enlarge the pipeline.
