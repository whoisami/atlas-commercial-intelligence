# KPIs — Market Opportunity

## Outcome measures

- Share of GO recommendations later validated by product and buyer evidence
- Median time from brief to decision-ready report
- Percent of major claims supported by E1/E2 evidence
- False-positive rate after CEO review
- Value of opportunities advanced to qualified meetings

## Guardrails

- Invented or unsupported decisive facts: **0**
- Unauthorized external contacts or commitments: **0**
- Records without source URLs: **0**
- Binding decisions created outside Atlas CEO: **0**

## Review cadence

Atlas CEO reviews these metrics at sprint close. Learning Intelligence compares prediction quality and process defects across completed sprints.
