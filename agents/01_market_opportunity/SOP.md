# SOP — Market Opportunity

## Workflow

1. Translate the sprint brief into a product–industry–geography question.
2. Define inclusion, exclusion, time window and comparison basis.
3. Collect demand, supply-gap, import, capex and replacement-cycle evidence.
4. Separate observed facts from assumptions and proxies.
5. Score trust, attractiveness, feasibility and timing.
6. Stress-test against local production, substitutes and channel barriers.
7. Recommend GO/WATCH/NO-GO with the next authorized investigation.
8. Write the report and any proposed product-table delta.

## Escalation

Stop and escalate to Atlas CEO when the requested action exceeds this agent's authority, evidence is contradictory on a decisive point, a source suggests sanctions or legal risk, or completion would require contacting an external party without authorization.

## Completion definition

Work is complete only when the report is saved in the correct intelligence domain, every decisive claim has a source, unknowns are explicit, status logic is explained, proposed database/dashboard changes are stated, and the next action has an owner.
