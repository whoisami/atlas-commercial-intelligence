# Inputs — Manufacturer Hunter

## Mandatory Product Intelligence handoff

- Accepted `intelligence/products/RXXX.md` with GO.
- Product scope and exclusions.
- Required variants.
- Must-have specifications and technical capabilities.
- Required certifications and documentation.
- Priority applications and industries.
- OEM versus aftermarket priority.
- Stock, MOQ and lead-time requirements.
- Remote-support and local-service expectations.
- Technical risks and open verification questions.

If the Product Intelligence handoff is absent or not GO, stop.

## Discovery sources

Prefer:

- Official manufacturer websites
- Official factory and company pages
- Technical catalogues and manuals
- Certification documents and databases
- National trade agencies
- Industry associations
- Trade-fair exhibitor lists
- Regulatory or approved-vendor sources
- Corporate filings and official registries
- LinkedIn company pages and verified employees
- Credible industry publications

Use marketplaces, generic directories and reseller pages only as lead or contradiction sources.

## Mandatory manufacturer fields

Every evaluated manufacturer must contain:

- Company
- Country
- Website
- Products
- Product Families
- Manufacturer Evidence
- Factory Evidence
- Estimated Employees
- Year Founded
- Export Evidence
- Export Countries
- ISO / CE / API Certifications
- LinkedIn Company
- Export Contact
- Export Email
- Sales Email
- General Email
- Phone
- Source URLs
- Turkey Presence
- Existing Turkey Distributor
- Atlas Notes

Use `UNKNOWN` for unverified values.

## Input verification rules

- Confirm the company and factory belong to the same entity.
- Confirm products are made, not merely stocked or resold.
- Confirm certificate scope and currency when material.
- Confirm export countries from explicit evidence.
- Confirm contact details from first-party sources.
- Confirm distributor authorization from first-party evidence.
- Record access date and evidence level.
- Preserve conflicting evidence rather than selecting the convenient version.

## Regional research depth

- Tier 1: full systematic search.
- Tier 2: targeted expansion and comparison.
- Tier 3: selective search; enhanced verification for China.
- Outside tiers: include only when a company has exceptional evidenced fit and explain why.
