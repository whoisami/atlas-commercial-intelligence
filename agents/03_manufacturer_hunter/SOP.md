# SOP — Manufacturer Hunter

## Workflow

1. Convert product criteria into manufacturer search strings and rejection rules.
2. Search official sites, catalogues, certifications and trade-agency sources.
3. Verify physical production and distinguish own-made from distributed products.
4. Verify export orientation and international customer evidence.
5. Search manufacturer and Turkish-language sources for channel conflicts.
6. Capture contacts only when verified; otherwise use UNKNOWN.
7. Apply trust, commercial and Atlas scores consistently.
8. Write ranked report and manufacturer delta for CEO review.

## Escalation

Stop and escalate to Atlas CEO when the requested action exceeds this agent's authority, evidence is contradictory on a decisive point, a source suggests sanctions or legal risk, or completion would require contacting an external party without authorization.

## Completion definition

Work is complete only when the report is saved in the correct intelligence domain, every decisive claim has a source, unknowns are explicit, status logic is explained, proposed database/dashboard changes are stated, and the next action has an owner.
