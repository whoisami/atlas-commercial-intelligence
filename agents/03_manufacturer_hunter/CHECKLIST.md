# Checklist — Manufacturer Hunter

## Start gate

- [ ] Accepted Product Intelligence report exists.
- [ ] Product status is GO.
- [ ] Product scope and exclusions are explicit.
- [ ] Required variants and specifications are explicit.
- [ ] Required certifications are explicit.
- [ ] Stock, lead-time and support expectations are explicit.
- [ ] Manufacturer Hunter is not redefining the product.

## Search strategy

- [ ] Tier 1 countries were searched systematically.
- [ ] Tier 2 expansion was justified.
- [ ] Tier 3 research was selective.
- [ ] China candidates received enhanced verification.
- [ ] Country tier did not replace company-level evidence.
- [ ] Search terms covered product synonyms, standards, applications and processes.
- [ ] A stopping rule prevented list inflation.

## Manufacturer verification

- [ ] Company identity and website are verified.
- [ ] Relevant products are verified.
- [ ] Own manufacturing is supported by E1 evidence.
- [ ] Factory evidence belongs to the same entity.
- [ ] Own-made and distributed products are separated.
- [ ] Technical capability matches Product Intelligence requirements.
- [ ] Estimated employees have a source basis or are UNKNOWN.
- [ ] Year founded is verified or UNKNOWN.
- [ ] Fake, copied or contradictory claims were checked.
- [ ] Distributors, traders, marketplaces and OEM resellers were rejected.

## Export verification

- [ ] Export capability has E1/E2 evidence.
- [ ] Export countries are listed only when explicit.
- [ ] English-language presence is not the sole export proof.
- [ ] International standards and documentation are assessed.
- [ ] Official export, sales or general contact route is verified.
- [ ] Manufacturers without credible export capability are rejected.

## Certifications

- [ ] ISO / CE / API claims are relevant to the product.
- [ ] Certificate scope, issuer and date were checked where material.
- [ ] Logos alone were not treated as verification.
- [ ] Expired, ambiguous or unverifiable certificates are identified.
- [ ] UNKNOWN is used instead of inference.

## Turkey channel

- [ ] Official partner/distributor pages were searched.
- [ ] Manufacturer site was searched for Turkey and Türkiye.
- [ ] Turkish-language sources were searched.
- [ ] Reseller presence is separated from authorization.
- [ ] Existing Turkey distributor is evidenced or UNKNOWN/NONE FOUND.
- [ ] “None found” is not presented as proof of absence.
- [ ] Strong established Turkey presence results in NO-GO.

## Contact verification

- [ ] LinkedIn company page matches the entity.
- [ ] Export contact is verified or UNKNOWN.
- [ ] Export email is verified or UNKNOWN.
- [ ] Sales email is verified or UNKNOWN.
- [ ] General email is verified or UNKNOWN.
- [ ] Phone is verified or UNKNOWN.
- [ ] No email pattern or title was inferred.

## Commercial evaluation

- [ ] Export Readiness is scored 0–100.
- [ ] Turkey Expansion Potential is scored 0–100.
- [ ] Communication Quality is scored 0–100.
- [ ] Technical Strength is scored 0–100.
- [ ] Commercial Fit is scored 0–100.
- [ ] Representation Potential is scored 0–100.
- [ ] Risk is scored 0–100 with high meaning severe.
- [ ] Trust is scored 0–100.
- [ ] Inverse Risk is calculated correctly.
- [ ] Atlas Score weights total 100%.
- [ ] Every score has evidence and uncertainty.
- [ ] Partnership willingness is labeled as hypothesis unless directly evidenced.

## GO gate

- [ ] Atlas Score ≥ 80.
- [ ] Export Readiness ≥ 70.
- [ ] Representation Potential ≥ 70.
- [ ] Trust ≥ 75.
- [ ] Manufacturer and factory evidence are verified.
- [ ] Product fit is verified.
- [ ] Export capability is verified.
- [ ] Official contact route is verified.
- [ ] No strong Turkey channel is evident.
- [ ] No fatal risk remains.

If any box fails, the manufacturer cannot be GO.

## Portfolio quality

- [ ] Top 10 contains no NO-GO company.
- [ ] Top 5 emphasizes partner probability, not fame.
- [ ] Top 3 contains only GO candidates.
- [ ] Top 3 has an official contact route.
- [ ] Top 3 has an Atlas value hypothesis and first-message angle.
- [ ] Rankings use identical scoring rules.
- [ ] List size did not substitute for evidence quality.

## Outputs

- [ ] Report is saved as the next `intelligence/manufacturers/RXXX.md`.
- [ ] Report ends with the five mandatory sections in order.
- [ ] `database/manufacturers.csv` matches the report.
- [ ] `workspace/active_project.md` matches shortlist and next gate.
- [ ] Only Manufacturer Hunter dashboard values were updated.
- [ ] Stable IDs, unrelated content and source history were preserved.
- [ ] Due-diligence packets exist for Top 3.
- [ ] Every unknown states what must be verified next.

## Integrity

- [ ] No company, factory, employee, date, export market, certification, contact or relationship was invented.
- [ ] Every recommended company has source evidence.
- [ ] Search-result pages are not final sources.
- [ ] No external outreach or negotiation was performed.
