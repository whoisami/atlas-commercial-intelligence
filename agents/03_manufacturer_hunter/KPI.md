# KPIs — Manufacturer Hunter

## North-star outcome

**Share of Top 3 Immediate Outreach Candidates that pass due diligence and progress to a qualified commercial conversation with realistic partnership potential.**

The agent is not rewarded for finding more companies.

## Discovery-quality KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target |
|---|---|---:|
| Manufacturer classification accuracy | Recommended entities confirmed as own-product manufacturers | 100% |
| Factory verification coverage | GO candidates with verified factory evidence | 100% |
| Export verification coverage | GO candidates with E1/E2 export evidence | 100% |
| Source completeness | Mandatory fields supported or explicitly UNKNOWN | 100% |
| Duplicate-entity rate | Duplicate legal entities or brands entered separately without explanation | 0 |
| False manufacturer rate | Distributors/traders incorrectly advanced | 0 |

## Partnership-quality KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target |
|---|---|---:|
| Top 3 due-diligence pass rate | Top 3 candidates cleared by Manufacturer Due Diligence | ≥ 70% |
| Top 3 contactability | Top 3 with verified official contact route | 100% |
| Representation thesis coverage | GO candidates with evidence-backed Atlas value hypothesis | 100% |
| Turkey conflict escape rate | GO candidates later found to have publicly visible strong Turkey channels | ≤ 5% |
| Qualified conversation rate | Top 3 candidates reaching a substantive commercial reply or meeting after authorized outreach | Track by sprint; improve quarterly |
| Agreement progression | GO candidates progressing to proposal, pilot or representation discussion | Track by cohort |

## Commercial evaluation KPIs

- GO candidates with Atlas Score ≥ 80: **100%**
- GO candidates with Export Readiness ≥ 70: **100%**
- GO candidates with Representation Potential ≥ 70: **100%**
- GO candidates with Trust ≥ 75: **100%**
- Scorecards with evidence and uncertainty for every dimension: **100%**
- Atlas Score arithmetic errors: **0**

## Search-efficiency KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target |
|---|---|---:|
| Time to decision-ready portfolio | Product Intelligence GO to final Manufacturer Hunter report | ≤ 10 business days unless scope requires otherwise |
| Rejection efficiency | Non-manufacturers and clear conflicts rejected before deep scoring | ≥ 90% |
| Deep-review yield | Deep-reviewed candidates reaching GO or bounded WATCH | ≥ 60% |
| Excess research after portfolio sufficiency | Hours spent after defensible Top 10/5/3 exists | 0 avoidable hours |
| Low-value list inflation | Candidates added only to increase count | 0 |

## Data-quality KPIs

- Recommended companies without source URLs: **0**
- Invented contacts, emails, certifications, dates or export countries: **0**
- Unknown values silently estimated: **0**
- Authorization inferred from reseller pages: **0**
- Stale decision-critical evidence without warning: **0**
- China GO candidates without enhanced verification: **0**

## Downstream feedback

Manufacturer Due Diligence records:

- Missed identity or ownership conflicts.
- Unsupported factory claims.
- Certificate failures.
- Export-evidence failures.
- Turkey-channel conflicts.
- Contact verification failures.
- Partnership-thesis weaknesses.

Outreach Intelligence records:

- Bounce and contact validity.
- Response quality.
- Evidence of existing representation.
- Reasons manufacturers reject Atlas.

These outcomes recalibrate scoring through Learning Intelligence.

## Guardrails

- Unauthorized outreach or negotiation: **0**
- Commercial commitments or exclusivity promises: **0**
- Manufacturer selection outside the Product Intelligence scope: **0**
- Changes to another agent's files or dashboard values: **0**
- NO-GO companies appearing in Top 10: **0**

## Review cadence

- Validate all GO gates before report closure.
- Review Top 3 outcomes after due diligence.
- Review response and meeting outcomes after authorized outreach.
- Recalibrate country assumptions and score weights quarterly using evidence, not anecdotes.
