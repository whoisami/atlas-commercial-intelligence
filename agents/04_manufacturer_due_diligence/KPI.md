# KPIs — Manufacturer Due Diligence

## North-star outcome

**Preventable post-contact and post-agreement manufacturer failures avoided through accurate pre-contact diligence.**

The agent is rewarded for eliminating weak counterparties and clearing strong ones with explicit controls—not for approving more companies.

## Verification KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target |
|---|---|---:|
| Legal-identity verification | Cases with resolved legal entity and active status before decision | 100% |
| Manufacturing re-verification | GO cases with independently re-verified manufacturing and factory evidence | 100% |
| Export verification | GO cases with independently verified export capability | 100% |
| Certification verification | Decision-critical certificates verified by scope and validity | 100% |
| Sanctions/trade-screen coverage | Cases screened through appropriate authoritative sources | 100% |
| Turkey-channel verification | Cases with evidenced channel classification and limitations | 100% |
| Critical unknown closure | Decision-critical gaps closed before GO | 100% |

## Risk-decision KPIs

- GO cases with Overall Due Diligence Risk ≤ 30: **100%**
- GO cases with Fraud Risk ≤ 20: **100%**
- GO cases with Representation Risk ≤ 30: **100%**
- GO cases with Brand Risk ≤ 30: **100%**
- GO cases with Evidence Quality ≥ 80: **100%**
- Risk-score arithmetic errors: **0**
- Fatal risks averaged away: **0**

## Outcome KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target |
|---|---|---:|
| Post-contact surprise rate | Material adverse facts discoverable before contact but missed | ≤ 5% |
| Due-diligence false-GO rate | GO later rejected for evidence available at decision time | ≤ 5% |
| Due-diligence false-NO-GO rate | Rejected candidate later shown viable because evidence was misread | Track and minimize |
| WATCH resolution rate | WATCH cases reaching definitive GO/NO-GO within deadline | ≥ 80% |
| Contact-readiness acceptance | GO cases accepted by Atlas CEO/Outreach without major rework | ≥ 90% |
| Channel-conflict miss rate | GO cases later found to have public strong Turkey representation | 0 |

## Commercial-protection KPIs

- Fraud or impersonation losses after GO: **0**
- Sanctions/trade violations after GO: **0**
- Payment exposure outside recommended controls: **0**
- Brand-damaging partnerships attributable to missed public evidence: **0**
- Supply failures attributable to ignored diligence evidence: **0**
- Unauthorized contact before GO: **0**

## Efficiency KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target |
|---|---|---:|
| Time to due-diligence decision | Complete Hunter packet to GO/WATCH/NO-GO | ≤ 5 business days unless registry/sanctions access blocks |
| Early rejection efficiency | Fatal failures identified before full scoring | ≥ 90% |
| Verification-gap specificity | WATCH gaps with evidence required, owner and deadline | 100% |
| Duplicate research | Rework caused by uncited or unstructured evidence | 0 |
| Open-ended WATCH cases | WATCH without review trigger | 0 |

## Data-quality KPIs

- Records without source URLs: **0**
- Invented or inferred legal, financial, sanctions, customer, export or contact facts: **0**
- UNKNOWN values without commercial impact and next verification: **0**
- Stale decision-critical evidence without warning: **0**
- Discovery scores overwritten by diligence risk scores: **0**

## Feedback loop

Atlas CEO and Outreach Intelligence must record:

- Whether the contact route was valid.
- Whether the manufacturer disclosed an existing Turkey channel.
- Whether ownership, export, certification or financial claims changed.
- Whether commercial behavior matched the risk assessment.
- Why the manufacturer advanced, stalled or was rejected.

Learning Intelligence uses these outcomes to recalibrate risk thresholds and controls.

## Review cadence

- Validate every GO gate before report closure.
- Review WATCH deadlines weekly.
- Review GO outcomes after first response, first meeting and any proposal.
- Review risk-model calibration quarterly.
- Change thresholds only through a documented governance decision.
