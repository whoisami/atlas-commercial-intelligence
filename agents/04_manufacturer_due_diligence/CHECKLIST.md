# Checklist — Manufacturer Due Diligence

## Start gate

- [ ] Manufacturer Hunter report is accepted.
- [ ] Candidate is GO for due diligence.
- [ ] Stable manufacturer ID exists.
- [ ] Product Intelligence requirements are available.
- [ ] Intended contact action is defined.
- [ ] Evidence packet and diligence questions are complete.
- [ ] No external contact is required to begin.

## Entity resolution

- [ ] Legal existence is verified.
- [ ] Legal name, aliases and jurisdiction are resolved.
- [ ] Registration and active status are verified.
- [ ] Registered and operating addresses are distinguished.
- [ ] Ownership and group structure are verified or UNKNOWN.
- [ ] Website, email domain, phone and address are consistent.
- [ ] Similar-name and impersonation risk is resolved.
- [ ] Years in business is sourced.
- [ ] Employee estimate has a source basis or is UNKNOWN.

## Manufacturing and product

- [ ] Own-manufacturer status is independently re-verified.
- [ ] Factory evidence belongs to the legal entity.
- [ ] Production processes and testing capability are verified.
- [ ] Owned, subcontracted, assembled and resold products are separated.
- [ ] Product claims are consistent across sources.
- [ ] Product fit matches Product Intelligence.
- [ ] Supply continuity and capacity signals are assessed.
- [ ] Technical contradictions and quality concerns are recorded.

## Certifications

- [ ] Required certifications are identified.
- [ ] Issuer, certificate number, entity, site and scope are checked.
- [ ] Validity date is checked where possible.
- [ ] Management-system and product certifications are distinguished.
- [ ] Logo-only, expired or ambiguous claims are not accepted.
- [ ] Missing certification impact is stated.

## Export and international capability

- [ ] Export capability is independently verified.
- [ ] Export markets are sourced.
- [ ] International customers are sourced or UNKNOWN.
- [ ] Export documentation and language capability are assessed.
- [ ] Export department is verified or UNKNOWN.
- [ ] Logistics, returns, warranty and service capability are assessed.
- [ ] English website alone is not export proof.

## Financial and commercial stability

- [ ] Official financial/filing evidence was checked where available.
- [ ] Insolvency or dissolution signals were checked.
- [ ] Financial figures are dated and sourced.
- [ ] Financial proxies are labeled as proxies.
- [ ] Creditworthiness is not invented.
- [ ] Ownership or customer concentration risk is considered.
- [ ] Payment controls are defined when evidence is limited.
- [ ] Commercial stability conclusion is evidence-based.

## Contacts and communication

- [ ] LinkedIn company presence matches the entity.
- [ ] Official response channels are verified.
- [ ] Named contact, title and email are independently verified or UNKNOWN.
- [ ] Export department is verified or UNKNOWN.
- [ ] Contact quality is scored with evidence.
- [ ] Domain inconsistency and impersonation risk are checked.
- [ ] No message was sent.

## Turkey channel

- [ ] Manufacturer partner/distributor sources were checked.
- [ ] Turkey/Türkiye searches were performed.
- [ ] Turkish-language sources were checked.
- [ ] Reseller presence is separated from authorization.
- [ ] Existing distributor status is evidenced or UNKNOWN/NONE FOUND.
- [ ] Direct sales and multi-channel behavior are considered.
- [ ] Potential channel conflict is scored and explained.
- [ ] Material unresolved conflict blocks GO.

## Sanctions and trade restrictions

- [ ] Legal names and known aliases were screened.
- [ ] Owners were screened when known and relevant.
- [ ] Current authoritative sanctions sources were used.
- [ ] Sources and screening date are recorded.
- [ ] Potential matches are resolved or escalated.
- [ ] Product/end-use/export-control issues were considered.
- [ ] Country, banking and payment-route restrictions were considered.
- [ ] A generic web search was not treated as sanctions clearance.
- [ ] Unresolved material issue results in NO-GO pending qualified review.

## Reputation and brand

- [ ] Credible adverse-media sources were checked.
- [ ] Litigation, recalls, quality failures and regulatory actions were considered.
- [ ] Allegations are separated from verified facts.
- [ ] Positive and negative evidence are balanced.
- [ ] Brand impact on Atlas is explicitly assessed.

## Risk scoring

- [ ] Commercial Risk is scored 0–100.
- [ ] Fraud Risk is scored 0–100.
- [ ] Supply Risk is scored 0–100.
- [ ] Communication Risk is scored 0–100.
- [ ] Political Risk is scored 0–100.
- [ ] Logistics Risk is scored 0–100.
- [ ] Payment Risk is scored 0–100.
- [ ] Representation Risk is scored 0–100.
- [ ] Brand Risk is scored 0–100.
- [ ] Higher always means greater risk.
- [ ] Weights total 100%.
- [ ] Overall Due Diligence Risk arithmetic is verified.
- [ ] Evidence Quality is scored separately.
- [ ] Every score has evidence, confidence, gaps and controls.
- [ ] Fatal risk is not averaged away.

## GO gate

- [ ] Overall Due Diligence Risk ≤ 30.
- [ ] Fraud Risk ≤ 20.
- [ ] Representation Risk ≤ 30.
- [ ] Brand Risk ≤ 30.
- [ ] Evidence Quality ≥ 80.
- [ ] Legal existence is verified.
- [ ] Manufacturing and factory are verified.
- [ ] Product consistency is verified.
- [ ] Export capability is verified.
- [ ] Required certifications are resolved.
- [ ] Official response channel is verified.
- [ ] Sanctions/trade screening is complete.
- [ ] No material Turkey-channel conflict exists.
- [ ] No decision-critical verification gap remains.
- [ ] Required controls are practical.

If any box fails, GO is prohibited.

## Output quality

- [ ] Report uses the next `intelligence/manufacturers/RXXX.md`.
- [ ] Report ends with GO/WATCH/NO-GO.
- [ ] All six mandatory commercial-action fields are present.
- [ ] Every recommendation cites evidence.
- [ ] Every UNKNOWN explains impact and next verification.
- [ ] `database/manufacturers.csv` matches the report.
- [ ] `workspace/active_project.md` matches status and next gate.
- [ ] Only assigned dashboard values were updated.
- [ ] Stable IDs, prior evidence and unrelated content were preserved.
- [ ] GO is routed for contact preparation, not contact execution.

## Integrity

- [ ] No legal, financial, sanctions, customer, export, contact or risk data was invented.
- [ ] No legal or credit opinion exceeds the evidence.
- [ ] No manufacturer was contacted.
- [ ] No risk was suppressed for commercial convenience.
