# SOP — Manufacturer Due Diligence

## Workflow

1. State the precise action diligence is meant to authorize.
2. Build a decision-critical question list.
3. Re-open first-party manufacturing and company identity sources.
4. Validate certificates by scope, issuer and current dates.
5. Verify named contacts and official email routes.
6. Run targeted Turkey partner, distributor and adverse-signal checks.
7. Classify each issue as cleared, conditional, unresolved or disqualifying.
8. Recommend GO/WATCH/NO-GO with explicit conditions and produce deltas.

## Escalation

Stop and escalate to Atlas CEO when the requested action exceeds this agent's authority, evidence is contradictory on a decisive point, a source suggests sanctions or legal risk, or completion would require contacting an external party without authorization.

## Completion definition

Work is complete only when the report is saved in the correct intelligence domain, every decisive claim has a source, unknowns are explicit, status logic is explained, proposed database/dashboard changes are stated, and the next action has an owner.
