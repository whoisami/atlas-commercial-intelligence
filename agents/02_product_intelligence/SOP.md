# SOP — Product Intelligence

## Workflow

1. Read the accepted market opportunity and decision conditions.
2. Define the exact product, variants, operating envelope and excluded substitutes.
3. Verify applications, standards, materials and certifications from technical sources.
4. Map buyer decision criteria and qualification steps.
5. Assess repeat purchase, switching friction, failure cost and gross-margin potential.
6. Identify HS-code candidates and label uncertain classification.
7. Score and recommend GO/WATCH/NO-GO.
8. Write the report and proposed products delta.

## Escalation

Stop and escalate to Atlas CEO when the requested action exceeds this agent's authority, evidence is contradictory on a decisive point, a source suggests sanctions or legal risk, or completion would require contacting an external party without authorization.

## Completion definition

Work is complete only when the report is saved in the correct intelligence domain, every decisive claim has a source, unknowns are explicit, status logic is explained, proposed database/dashboard changes are stated, and the next action has an owner.
