# KPIs — Product Intelligence

## North-star outcome

**Share of Product Intelligence GO reports that enable Manufacturer Hunter to produce a technically valid shortlist without redefining the product or requesting major clarification.**

## Product-definition KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target |
|---|---|---:|
| Manufacturer-handoff acceptance | GO handoffs accepted without major product-scope rework | ≥ 90% |
| Specification completeness | Decision-critical selection fields identified and sourced | ≥ 95% |
| Variant coverage | Material commercial variants mapped before manufacturer research | ≥ 90% |
| Application traceability | Priority applications linked to variant and specification requirements | 100% |
| Critical unknown closure | Decision-critical unknowns closed before GO | 100% |

## Commercial-understanding KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target |
|---|---|---:|
| Buying-center completeness | User, specifier, approver, buyer and payer mapped or explicitly UNKNOWN | 100% |
| Demand-route clarity | OEM, project, aftermarket and MRO routes separated | 100% |
| Repeat-purchase validation | GO products with sourced recurrence mechanism | 100% |
| Stock-free feasibility | GO products with an explicit operational no-stock route | 100% |
| Remote-support readiness | GO products with remote/local support boundary defined | 100% |
| Value-proposition coverage | Value stated for user, specifier, procurement and payer | 100% |

## Technical-risk KPIs

- GO reports with critical failure consequences identified: **100%**
- Mandatory certifications distinguished from optional approvals: **100%**
- GTIP/HS candidates with confidence and verification route: **100%**
- Product-liability or safety surprises caused by missed public evidence: **0**
- Provisional codes presented as final: **0**

## Downstream outcome KPIs

- Manufacturer shortlist technical-match rate.
- Manufacturer rejection rate caused by incorrect product requirements.
- Buyer discovery rework caused by incomplete application mapping.
- Pricing-analysis rework caused by missing specification or order-unit fields.
- Time from Product Intelligence GO to five diligence-ready manufacturers.
- Time from Product Intelligence GO to a technically qualified buyer conversation.

## Efficiency KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target |
|---|---|---:|
| Time to product-readiness decision | Accepted market GO to Product Intelligence GO/WATCH/NO-GO | ≤ 7 business days unless standards access blocks |
| Duplicate technical research | Repeated work caused by undocumented sources or taxonomy | 0 |
| Open-ended research | Reports without a bounded next decision | 0 |
| Manufacturer research started before product GO | Process violations | 0 |

## Scoring integrity

- Reports with all nine raw scores: **100%**
- Scores with evidence, confidence and interpretation: **100%**
- Commercial Product Fit arithmetic errors: **0**
- Complexity burden mislabeled as attractiveness: **0**

## Guardrails

- Invented product facts, codes, certifications, quantities, MOQs or intervals: **0**
- Unsupported compliance or engineering advice: **0**
- Manufacturer ranking performed by this agent: **0**
- Unauthorized outreach or commercial commitments: **0**
- Changes to another agent's files or dashboard values: **0**

## Review cadence

- Review checklist and scoring before every report closes.
- Manufacturer Hunter records handoff defects when discovered.
- Buyer and Pricing Intelligence record missing product fields that caused rework.
- Atlas CEO reviews product-readiness accuracy at sprint close.
- Learning Intelligence recalibrates the framework from observed outcomes, never from anecdote alone.
