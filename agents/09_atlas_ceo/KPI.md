# KPIs — Atlas CEO

## North-star outcome

**Long-term recurring commercial value created per unit of Atlas time and capital.**

Atlas CEO is not measured by reports, research volume or number of active opportunities.

## Commercial outcome metrics

- Manufacturer meetings
- Manufacturer response rate
- Buyer conversations
- Pilot projects
- Quotations
- Orders
- Revenue
- Commission earned
- Recurring revenue

Every metric must have a source, period and definition.

## Milestone-conversion KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Outreach-to-manufacturer-meeting | Qualified meetings / authorized manufacturer outreach | Meeting effectiveness |
| Meeting-to-pilot | Pilots / qualified manufacturer meetings | Partner progression |
| Pilot-to-quotation | Quotations / pilots | Commercial validation |
| Quotation-to-order | Orders / quotations | Sales conversion |
| Order-to-recurring | Repeat-revenue accounts / first-order accounts | Long-term quality |
| Time to first meeting | Decision GO to qualified meeting | Speed |
| Time to first revenue | Decision GO to recognized revenue | Speed |

## Decision-quality KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target |
|---|---|---:|
| Time from sufficient evidence to decision | Accepted evidence to binding decision | ≤ 1 business day |
| Decision reversal from ignored evidence | Decisions reversed due to evidence available at decision time | ≤ 5% |
| GO milestone success | GO decisions reaching their defined next milestone | ≥ 70% |
| WATCH resolution | WATCH decisions resolved by review date | ≥ 80% |
| Kill timeliness | Weak projects stopped when trigger is reached | 100% |
| Action ownership | Actions with owner, due date and completion evidence | 100% |

## Resource-efficiency KPIs

- Active primary opportunities: **1 unless capacity is explicitly assigned**
- Research tasks without a named decision impact: **0**
- Opportunities exceeding contact caps: **0**
- Capital deployed without explicit cap and downside: **0**
- Open actions without owner/deadline: **0**
- Killed-project resources released promptly: **100%**

## Confidence calibration

Track actual outcomes by CEO Confidence Score:

- Manufacturer response
- Meeting
- Pilot
- Quotation
- Order
- Revenue timing
- Revenue amount/range

High-confidence decisions should outperform lower-confidence decisions. Recalibrate with Learning Intelligence; do not manipulate confidence after outcomes.

## Portfolio KPIs

- Expected value concentration by opportunity
- Capital exposure by opportunity
- Time exposure by opportunity
- Number of WATCH opportunities
- Number of killed opportunities
- Next-best opportunity readiness
- Recurring-revenue share
- Commission versus trading revenue mix

## Guardrails

- Research or web searches performed by CEO: **0**
- Invented facts, probabilities, revenue or timing: **0**
- GO decisions without accepted evidence: **0**
- Outreach authorized without Due Diligence and Outreach GO: **0**
- Stock/exclusivity authorized without pricing/competition evidence: **0**
- Weak projects kept alive past kill trigger: **0**
- Binding decisions created by another agent: **0**
- Operating surfaces in conflict after decision: **0**

## Review cadence

### Weekly

- Commercial targets
- Responses and meetings
- Next actions
- Blockers
- Kill triggers
- Resource caps

### Sprint close

- Milestone progress
- Decision calibration
- Time/capital use
- Continue/change/kill decisions
- Lessons for Learning Intelligence

### Monthly

- Revenue, commission and recurring pipeline
- Opportunity concentration
- Model performance
- Portfolio reprioritization

Execution metrics must lead to decisions, not dashboard decoration.
