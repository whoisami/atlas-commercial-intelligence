## Market and competitive sources

Use lawfully available sources such as:

- Turkish official trade and production statistics
- Customs and GTIP/HS sources
- Industry associations and chambers
- Official company and factory sites
- Manufacturer partner/distributor locators
- Turkish distributor and representative sites
- Public tenders and procurement results
- Buyer facility and approved-vendor evidence
- Public project and investment announcements
- Technical catalogues and certification sources
- Public price, tender and accepted Pricing Intelligence evidence
- Trade-fair and association records
- Credible industry publications
- Professional company pages

## Evidence-state rules

Every material claim must be:

- VERIFIED
- ESTIMATED
- UNKNOWN

For ESTIMATED claims record method, source, date, range, confidence and sensitivity.

## Integrity rules

- Do not infer market share from website visibility.
- Do not infer authorization from seller claims alone.
- Do not call import value market size.
- Do not invent growth, loyalty or concentration.
- Use only public, lawful information.
- Record contradictory evidence.
- State what must be verified next.
