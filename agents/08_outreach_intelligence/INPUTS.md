# Inputs — Outreach Intelligence

## Mandatory manufacturer and contact inputs

- Manufacturer Due Diligence GO report
- Stable manufacturer ID
- Verified legal/company identity
- Verified Turkey-channel status
- Verified contact or official department
- Verified email, LinkedIn or phone source
- Contact verification date
- Prior contact history
- Do-not-contact status
- CEO authorization scope

If these are incomplete, use WATCH.

## Required commercial intelligence

- Accepted Product Intelligence report
- Accepted Buyer Intelligence report
- Accepted Pricing Intelligence report when using ROI, price or margin claims
- Accepted Competition Intelligence report
- Manufacturer Hunter partnership hypothesis
- Manufacturer Due Diligence controls
- Active project objective
- 90-day pilot constraints
- Approved Atlas positioning

## Evidence required for external claims

Obtain or mark UNKNOWN:

- Manufacturer-specific product/export fact
- Turkey relevance
- Turkey channel gap
- Buyer segment evidence
- Named buyer evidence, if used
- Commercial pain hypothesis
- Customer value driver
- Price/ROI/business-impact claim
- Pilot output and metric
- Contact role and channel
- Response/meeting probability basis
- Timing recommendation

## Evidence classification

Every important input is:

- VERIFIED
- ESTIMATED
- UNKNOWN

Do not use an ESTIMATED or UNKNOWN statement as an external fact.

## Public-information and privacy rules

- Use public professional/business information only.
- Do not infer email patterns or private phone numbers.
- Do not bypass access controls.
- Do not enrich with irrelevant personal details.
- Respect prior opt-out, rejection and do-not-contact records.
- Record source URL and verification date.

## Authorization rules

The agent may draft without send authorization. Sending, calling, connecting or publishing requires explicit human approval for:

- Recipient
- Message
- Channel
- Timing
- Attachment
- Follow-up step

Authorization for one step does not automatically authorize the sequence.
