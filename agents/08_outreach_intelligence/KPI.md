# KPIs — Outreach Intelligence

## North-star outcome

**Qualified manufacturer meetings generated per authorized outreach cohort without harming Atlas's brand or violating evidence and authorization controls.**

The agent is not measured by email volume.

## Readiness KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target |
|---|---|---:|
| Verified-recipient coverage | GO actions with verified recipient/department and channel | 100% |
| Personalization coverage | GO first touches with at least two relevant verified manufacturer facts | 100% |
| Evidence-state coverage | Important strategy claims labeled VERIFIED/ESTIMATED/UNKNOWN | 100% |
| Sequence completeness | GO cases with full channel, follow-up, objection, pilot and CRM assets | 100% |
| Authorization compliance | External steps executed with specific approval | 100% |
| Blocking-issue closure | GO cases with no unresolved blocker | 100% |

## Conversion KPIs

- Delivery rate
- Connection-acceptance rate
- Response rate
- Positive-response rate
- Qualified-response rate
- Meeting-request acceptance
- Meeting-scheduled rate
- Reply-to-meeting conversion
- Time to first response
- Time to meeting
- Pilot-discussion rate

Track by country, company size, export maturity, product type, role, channel and message angle. Do not compare tiny cohorts as if statistically conclusive.

## Prediction KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target |
|---|---|---:|
| Response-range calibration | Actual cohort response falls inside predicted range | ≥ 70% of mature cohorts |
| Meeting-range calibration | Actual cohort meeting rate falls inside predicted range | ≥ 70% of mature cohorts |
| Confidence accuracy | High-confidence GO actions outperform lower-confidence WATCH cases | Positive monotonic relationship |
| Timing accuracy | Recommended timing produces response without avoidable delay | Track and improve |
| Channel-fit accuracy | Recommended primary channel outperforms or explains deviation | Track by cohort |

## Message-quality KPIs

- Generic first messages: **0**
- Unsupported claims in approved messages: **0**
- First-message exclusivity requests: **0**
- Sales guarantees or overpromises: **0**
- Buyer claims without accepted evidence: **0**
- Personalization defects found after send: **0**
- Messages sounding like job applications: **0**

## Commercial-progress KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target |
|---|---|---:|
| Meetings advancing to next step | Meetings with agreed evidence request, pilot, proposal or follow-up | Track by cohort; improve quarterly |
| 90-day pilot interest | Qualified meetings considering pilot scope | Track |
| Partnership-path clarity | Positive replies classified by representation/commission/back-to-back/pilot route | 100% |
| Objection conversion | Objections converted to a proportionate next step | Track by objection |
| Respectful close rate | Clear rejections closed without damaging follow-up potential | 100% |

## CRM discipline KPIs

- Sent date recorded only after send: **100%**
- Responses classified within one business day: **100%**
- Follow-ups completed or explicitly paused: **100%**
- Do-not-contact/opt-out compliance: **100%**
- Open sequences without owner or next due date: **0**
- Duplicate outreach to same contact/opportunity: **0**

## Guardrails

- Unauthorized sends, calls or connection requests: **0**
- Invented contacts, facts, buyers, prices or results: **0**
- Private or inferred contact data used: **0**
- Deceptive identity or pretext: **0**
- Continued outreach after clear rejection/opt-out: **0**
- Other agents' files or metrics changed: **0**

## Feedback loop

Atlas CEO records:

- Authorization decision
- Actual send timing
- Delivery and response
- Objection
- Meeting result
- Pilot interest
- Next commercial step
- Reason for no response or rejection when known

Learning Intelligence recalibrates probability ranges, channels, timing, objections and pilot framing from actual cohorts.

## Review cadence

- Validate every GO gate before approval request.
- Review active sequences daily.
- Review cohort conversion monthly.
- Recalibrate probability estimates quarterly.
- Stop low-performing sequences when evidence supports a change; do not compensate with more volume.
