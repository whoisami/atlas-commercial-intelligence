# KPIs — Learning Intelligence

## North-star outcome

Evidence that adopted learning changes improve Atlas's progression from valid contact to meeting, proposal, order, commission and repeat revenue without increasing avoidable risk.

## Commercial performance metrics

| KPI | Definition |
|---|---|
| Response rate | Unique contacts with a human reply / unique valid contacts reached |
| Meeting conversion | Held qualified meetings / unique valid contacts reached |
| Proposal conversion | Issued proposals or quotations / held qualified meetings |
| Order conversion | Confirmed orders / issued proposals or quotations |
| Commission conversion | Commission-bearing orders / confirmed orders eligible for commission |
| Repeat business | Customers with a subsequent paid order / customers eligible to reorder in the window |
| Average sales cycle | Mean days from first authorized contact to confirmed order for closed-won records |
| Average response time | Mean business time from first valid contact to first human response among responders |

Every KPI must disclose numerator, denominator, cohort, time window and exclusions. Rankings by country, industry or product family must disclose sample size and the success outcome used.

## Learning-system KPIs

- percentage of lessons with complete mandatory fields;
- percentage of lessons supported by VERIFIED evidence;
- time from completed outcome to documented lesson;
- percentage of WATCH hypotheses resolved by their review date;
- percentage of adopted changes measured after implementation;
- percentage of validated patterns later retained, revised or retired;
- number of avoidable mistakes repeated after a validated lesson;
- measurable uplift or deterioration after playbook changes;
- number of obsolete or contradicted patterns retired.

## Quality targets

- Invented lessons, outcomes or denominators: **0**
- Unsupported causal claims: **0**
- Rates without numerator, denominator and period: **0**
- GO process changes below Confidence Score 80: **0**
- Playbook changes without evidence and rollback condition: **0**
- Country or industry generalizations beyond the observed cohort: **0**
- Unauthorized external contacts or commercial commitments: **0**
- Repeated avoidable mistakes after validated corrective action: target **0**

## Interpretation guardrails

- A higher reply rate is not automatically commercial success.
- A meeting is qualified only when the relevant commercial need and next step are recorded.
- Open opportunities do not count as losses or wins.
- Small cohorts are reported with counts and confidence limitations.
- Estimated metrics never replace verified financial outcomes.

## Review cadence

- After each material reply, meeting, quotation, order or loss: capture candidate lessons.
- Weekly during active outreach: review leading indicators and unresolved hypotheses.
- At sprint close: publish the learning report and process decisions.
- Quarterly or after a material strategy change: recalibrate patterns, playbooks and benchmark cohorts.
