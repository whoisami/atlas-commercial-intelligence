# Sprint 002 — SPETECH and Roten Contact Verification

## Executive Summary

- **SPETECH: GO.** Its official contact page publishes a dedicated export mailbox and four named people under the Export Department. A company LinkedIn page is verified. No official Turkey distributor or partner was found in the targeted public-source review.
- **Roten: WAIT.** Its official contact page verifies a commercial-office mailbox and its LinkedIn company page is valid, but no named export manager or international-sales contact was verified. A Turkish reseller offers Roten products while explicitly stating that it is not an official distributor or representative.
- **No contact data was inferred.** Where a person's exact title was not stated, the title remains `UNKNOWN`.

## SPETECH is ready for first contact

SPETECH's [official contact page](https://www.spetech.com.pl/pl/node/523) provides unusually strong first-party contact evidence. It publishes `export@spetech.com.pl` for the Export Department and lists four people beneath that heading:

- Wojciech Otrząsek — `wotrzasek@spetech.com.pl`
- Remigiusz Kuś — `rkus@spetech.com.pl`
- Marcin Lipowczan — `mlipowczan@spetech.com.pl`
- Elwira Zygmunt-Kociołek — `ezygmunt@spetech.com.pl`

The page does not state individual job titles, so no person is labeled Export Manager. Wojciech Otrząsek is used in the outreach CSV because he is the first named person under the official Export Department listing; his title remains `UNKNOWN`.

The [SPETECH LinkedIn company page](https://www.linkedin.com/company/spetech/) matches the manufacturer website, Bielsko-Biała headquarters and industrial sealing activity. It also shows active international-industry participation.

Targeted searches did not reveal a SPETECH-owned Turkish office, an authorized Turkey distributor, or an official Turkey partner announcement. That negative finding is not definitive because private or unpublished arrangements can exist. The first message should therefore ask whether Turkey is open and unassigned before presenting Atlas's market-entry proposal.

**Decision: GO.** There is a first-party export route, a named export-department contact, an active corporate profile and no visible official Turkey channel conflict.

## Roten needs one channel clarification

Roten's [official English contact page](https://www.roten-seals.com/contacts/?lang=en) verifies:

- Commercial Office — `commerciale@roten.it`
- General contact — `info@roten.it`

The page does not identify a named export manager, international-sales manager or commercial-office employee. Searches of the official site and public professional profiles did not produce a verifiable person-title combination. The outreach CSV therefore keeps both name and title as `UNKNOWN` and uses the official commercial-office mailbox.

The [ROTEN srl LinkedIn company page](https://www.linkedin.com/company/roten-srl/) matches the Milan address, website, mechanical-seal business and 1945 founding history.

A Turkish industrial reseller, [Yursat](https://www.yursat.com.tr/roten.html), advertises Roten products but explicitly states that it is not Roten's Turkey distributor or representative. No official Roten source naming a Turkish distributor or partner was found. Yursat is therefore evidence of Turkish spot supply, not evidence of an authorized channel.

**Decision: WAIT.** Roten has a verified sales mailbox and no confirmed official Turkey distributor, but Atlas should first ask the commercial office whether Turkey is open and who owns international sales. Promote to `GO` after a first-party answer confirms both points.

## Recommended Next Action

1. Send no outreach beyond a channel-qualification question.
2. For SPETECH, address Wojciech Otrząsek and copy `export@spetech.com.pl`; ask whether Turkey is currently assigned to a distributor, agent or employee.
3. For Roten, write to `commerciale@roten.it`; ask for the person responsible for exports and whether any company has authorized Turkey status.
4. Record the reply source and date before drafting a commercial representation pitch.

## Caveats

- Search review completed July 5, 2026 using public first-party pages, LinkedIn company pages and targeted Turkey-channel searches.
- “None found” does not prove that no private, non-exclusive or unpublished relationship exists.
- LinkedIn employee lists were not treated as proof of job responsibility without a matching role statement.
