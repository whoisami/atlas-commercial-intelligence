# Atlas Service Standard

Defines the official service standard of Atlas Commercial Intelligence.

## What Atlas is not

- A CRM
- A lead database
- Email automation software
- An ERP
- A generic consulting company

## What Atlas is

An **External Export & International Business Development Office** for manufacturing companies.

## Target client

Medium-sized manufacturers. Typical profile:

- Growth-oriented
- Exporting or export-ready
- No strong international business development team
- Wants to enter Europe and/or the Middle East

## Core mission

Help manufacturers make better commercial decisions and generate international business opportunities.

Atlas supports management decisions. Atlas does not replace management.

## Service portfolio

1. Commercial Opportunity Discovery
2. Buyer Intelligence
3. Sourcing Partner Discovery
4. Distributor Discovery
5. Market Entry Strategy
6. Export Growth Gap Analysis
7. Representation Opportunity Analysis
8. Commercial Opportunity Brief
9. Fractional Export Director
10. Monthly Export Board Review

## Email and communication

All communication must be documented. Store:

- Initial emails
- Follow-ups
- LinkedIn messages
- Meeting requests
- Meeting summaries
- Phone call notes
- Proposals
- Customer responses

## Board reporting

Every client receives:

- Executive Summary
- Commercial Status
- Pipeline
- Opportunities
- Risks
- Recommended Decisions
- 30-Day Plan
- 90-Day Strategy
- CEO Recommendations

## Atlas principles

- Evidence first.
- No invented facts.
- No generic recommendations.
- Every recommendation must support commercial growth.
- Every report must end with:
  - GO / WATCH / NO-GO
  - Immediate Next Action
  - Expected Business Impact

## Success metric

Atlas is successful only if it helps generate:

- Qualified meetings
- Commercial opportunities
- First orders
- Representation agreements
- Commission revenue
- Long-term business relationships
