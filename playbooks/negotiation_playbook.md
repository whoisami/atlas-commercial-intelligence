# Negotiation Playbook

Controlled Atlas standard for evidence-backed commercial negotiation.

## Current standard

- Define objectives, walk-away conditions and approval authority before negotiating.
- Separate price, scope, payment, territory, exclusivity, service and performance obligations.
- Trade concessions; do not give them without a reciprocal commercial gain.
- Record objections and terms accurately.
- Escalate legal, sanctions, channel-conflict and material payment risks.
