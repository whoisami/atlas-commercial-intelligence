# Email Playbook

Controlled Atlas standard for commercial email. Learning Intelligence updates this playbook only when recorded outcomes clear the GO evidence gate.

## Current standard

- Use verified recipient and company context.
- State one relevant commercial reason for contact.
- Make one low-friction next-step request.
- Do not invent traction, relationships, exclusivity or buyer demand.
- Follow authorization, cadence, privacy and stop rules.
