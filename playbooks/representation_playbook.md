# Representation Playbook

Controlled Atlas standard for representation, commission, market-development and later-stage exclusivity discussions.

## Current standard

- Begin with a bounded market-development or pilot scope.
- Define territory, products, lead ownership, commission event, payment timing and reporting.
- Validate manufacturer capacity, support and Turkey channel conflict before commitments.
- Tie exclusivity to measurable performance and review rights.
- Prefer reversible, stock-free entry until evidence supports capital exposure.
