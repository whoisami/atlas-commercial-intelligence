# Meeting Playbook

Controlled Atlas standard for manufacturer and buyer meetings. The objective is a qualified commercial next step, not a meeting count.

## Current standard

- Confirm purpose, participants and decision roles before the meeting.
- Test need, fit, timing, authority, commercial model and next gate.
- Record objections, commitments, unknowns, owner and due date.
- Distinguish scheduled, held and qualified meetings.
- Make no unauthorized commitment.
