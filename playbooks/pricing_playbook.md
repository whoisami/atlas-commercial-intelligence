# Pricing Playbook

Controlled Atlas standard for quotations, margins, commission and price learning.

## Current standard

- Label every input VERIFIED, ESTIMATED or UNKNOWN.
- Normalize currency, Incoterm, tax, freight, duty, payment and validity date.
- Maintain a gross-to-net bridge and scenario range.
- Protect cash flow, working capital and currency exposure.
- Never present an estimate as a manufacturer or market quote.
